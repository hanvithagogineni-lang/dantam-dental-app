Everything You Need to Know

Dental implants are titanium posts placed into the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots. They provide a strong foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures, restoring both function and appearance.

Most adults with healthy gums and adequate jawbone density are good candidates. Our implant specialists will evaluate your oral health and recommend the most suitable treatment plan.

Dental implant placement is performed under local anesthesia, ensuring a comfortable experience. Mild discomfort after the procedure is normal and typically subsides within a few days.

With proper oral hygiene and regular dental check-ups, dental implants can last for decades and, in many cases, a lifetime.

The cost depends on factors such as the number of implants, the implant system used, and whether additional procedures like bone grafting are required. A consultation provides a personalized treatment plan and cost estimate.

The treatment usually takes 3–6 months, depending on your healing process and whether additional procedures are needed before implant placement.
